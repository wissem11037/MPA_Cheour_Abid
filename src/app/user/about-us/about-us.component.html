Best Travel Tunisia, better known as BTT, was founded by a group of students in 2019.
At the start, the idea was conceived in a group project for school but later was fleshed out after two of the co-founders took a liking to it.
Today, BTT functions as a group of individuals coming together from different parts of the country having no official office, the company embraces the new way of work imposed by the pandemic and strives to be pioneers in this area of progress.
